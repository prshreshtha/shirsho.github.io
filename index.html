Music

Music is undoubtedly my biggest passion, and one of the very few things that I enjoy without the expectation of a reward.

I am a multi-instrumentalist/songwriter/producer and I handle the entire instrumental section of my latest musical endeavor, Transcend, a post rock duo that features my old bandmate Sifat Hasan.

Back in my high school days, I was also deeply involved with Absent Element, an experimental rock band from Dhaka, Bangladesh, where I participated in the capacity of a guitar player, songwriter, composer and lyricist.

We released two singles Pichutaan and Oprotiroddho in the compilation albums Aashor and Rock 505, released under Gaanchil and G-Series, two of the largest record labels in the country.
